## Design Principles
- **Professional & Trustworthy**: Color scheme conveys authority and reliability
- **Mobile-First**: Responsive design that works on all devices
- **Fast Loading**: Minimal dependencies, optimized assets


## Color Palette
- Primary: Navy blue (#1a365d) - Trust, professionalism
- Secondary: Gold/Bronze (#b8860b) - Excellence, prestige
- Accent: White (#ffffff) - Clean, clarity
- Text: Dark gray (#333333) - Readability
- Background: Light gray (#f8f9fa) - Subtle, professional

## Typography
- Headings: Georgia or serif fallback (traditional, authoritative)
- Body: System font stack (fast, readable)
