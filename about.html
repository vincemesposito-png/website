About Vincent M. Esposito

Over 40 years of zealous criminal defense representation.

Vincent M. Esposito

Criminal Defense Attorney | WA Bar #38566

With over 40 years of experience in criminal law at all levels of practice, I have dedicated my career to providing zealous and aggressive representation for those accused of crimes. My decades of experience mean I can handle even the most difficult cases with confidence and skill.

I believe in putting my client's interests first in every case. Whether you're facing a DUI, felony charges, misdemeanors, domestic violence accusations, drug crimes, or white collar offenses, I bring the same level of dedication and aggressive advocacy to achieve the best possible outcome.

Currently serving as an Associate of Imhoff and Associates, I also assist other law firms with their criminal cases. I am available for both virtual and in-person court appearances, and I represent clients on pre-file criminal matters as well.

My Philosophy

"I believe in zealous and aggressive representation with the interest of the client as the most paramount concern. Every person deserves a strong defense."

I take the time to truly understand each client's situation. No case is too small to receive my full attention, and no charge is too serious to fight. I approach every case with the same dedication and determination, because I know what's at stake for my clients and their families.

My fee arrangements are standard for criminal matters, but I am flexible based on personal factors and equity considerations. Everyone deserves quality representation.
