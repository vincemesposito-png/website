DUI / DWI Defense

A DUI charge can have devastating consequences: license suspension, hefty fines, increased insurance rates, and even jail time. I understand the science behind breathalyzer and field sobriety tests, and I know how to challenge them effectively.

My DUI defense strategies include:

  • Challenging the legality of the traffic stop
  • Questioning breathalyzer calibration and accuracy
  • Examining field sobriety test administration
  • Negotiating reduced charges or alternative sentencing
  • Fighting license suspension at DMV hearings
